Hot Chocolate

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream or marshmallows (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat the Milk:
    • In a saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until warm.
  2. Mix Cocoa and Sugar:
    • Stir in the cocoa powder and sugar until completely dissolved.
  3. Add Vanilla Extract:
    • Mix in the vanilla extract and stir well.
  4. Serve:
    • Pour into a mug and top with whipped cream or marshmallows, if desired.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!

Enjoy this cozy, comforting drink perfect for cold days!
