Fruit Popsicles

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pureed fruit (mango, berries, or watermelon)
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional)

Instructions

  1. Blend the Fruit:
    • Blend the fruit of your choice with honey (if using) until smooth.
  2. Pour into Molds:
    • Pour the mixture into popsicle molds.
  3. Freeze:
    • Freeze for at least 4 hours or until solid.
  4. Release the Popsicles:
    • Run warm water over the outside of the molds to release the popsicles.
  5. Enjoy your refreshing fruit popsicles!

These popsicles are a perfect treat to keep you cool during the summer heat!
