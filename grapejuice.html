Grape Juice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh grapes (preferably seedless)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1-2 tablespoons sugar or honey (optional, adjust to taste)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Wash the Grapes:
    • Wash the grapes thoroughly to remove any dirt or pesticides.
  2. Blend the Grapes:
    • Place the grapes in a blender and add 1/2 cup of water.
    • Blend until smooth, about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Sweeten the Juice:
    • If you prefer a sweeter juice, add sugar or honey to taste and blend again.
  4. Optional Strain:
    • For a smoother texture, strain the juice through a fine mesh sieve or cheesecloth to remove the pulp.
  5. Serve:
    • Serve immediately over ice cubes for a chilled drink, or refrigerate for later.

Enjoy the refreshing, naturally sweet taste of grape juice!
