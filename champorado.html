Champorado (Chocolate Rice Porridge)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup glutinous rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup condensed milk

Instructions

  1. Cook the Rice:
    • Cook glutinous rice in water until soft and thick.
  2. Add Cocoa Powder and Sugar:
    • Stir in cocoa powder and sugar, and cook for another 5 minutes.
  3. Finish with Condensed Milk:
    • Add condensed milk to achieve the desired creaminess.
  4. Serve:
    • Serve hot with extra sugar or milk.

Enjoy this warm, comforting treat perfect for rainy days!
