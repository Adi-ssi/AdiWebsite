Hot Pot (Shabu-Shabu)

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef or chicken (thinly sliced)
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bok choy or napa cabbage
  • 1 cup mushrooms (shiitake or button)
  • Soy sauce and sesame oil (for dipping sauce)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Broth:
    • Bring the vegetable broth to a boil in a hot pot or deep pot.
  2. Cook the Vegetables:
    • Add onions, mushrooms, and bok choy to the broth and simmer for a few minutes.
  3. Add the Meat:
    • Add thinly sliced beef or chicken and cook briefly in the broth.
  4. Serve:
    • Serve hot with dipping sauces like soy sauce and sesame oil.
  5. Enjoy:
    • Enjoy with rice or noodles.

This hot pot is perfect for sharing and is a warming treat for any rainy day!
