Baked Zucchini Chips

Ingredients

  • 1 zucchini
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Slice the zucchini thinly.
  3. Toss the zucchini slices with olive oil and seasonings.
  4. Arrange on a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until crispy.
