Pancit Malabon

Ingredients

  • 1 pack rice noodles (bihon or palabok)
  • 1/2 lb shrimp, peeled
  • 1/2 lb pork belly, sliced
  • 2 tbsp shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, sliced
  • 1/4 cup chicharrón (crushed pork skin)
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1/4 cup calamansi juice

Instructions

  1. Cook the Noodles:
    • Cook noodles according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. Sauté Pork and Shrimp:
    • In a pan, sauté onion and garlic, then add pork and shrimp. Cook until browned.
  3. Stir in Seasonings:
    • Stir in shrimp paste, fish sauce, and calamansi juice.
  4. Mix with Noodles:
    • Toss in the cooked noodles, then mix thoroughly.
  5. Top with Garnishes:
    • Top with hard-boiled eggs, chicharrón, and a squeeze of calamansi.
  6. Serve:
    • Serve warm.

Enjoy this savory and flavorful Filipino noodle dish!
