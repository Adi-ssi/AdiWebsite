Braised Pork

Ingredients

  • 1 kg (2.2 lbs) pork belly, cut into 2-inch cubes
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional, for added depth)
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar or honey
  • 2 cups water or low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 cinnamon stick (optional)
  • 2 star anise (optional)
  • 2-3 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (optional, for thickening)
  • Green onions (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pork:
    • Cut the pork belly into cubes. You can also use other cuts like pork shoulder or pork neck for a leaner option.
  2. Sear the Pork:
    • Heat the vegetable oil in a large, heavy-bottomed pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add the pork cubes and sear them on all sides until browned, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the pork from the pot and set it aside.
  3. Saut√© Aromatics:
    • In the same pot, add the chopped onion and garlic. Cook for about 2-3 minutes until softened and fragrant.
  4. Deglaze the Pot:
    • Add the soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), and brown sugar to the pot, stirring well to combine. Scrape any browned bits from the bottom of the pot with a wooden spoon for added flavor.
  5. Add Liquids and Spices:
    • Return the seared pork cubes to the pot. Add the water or broth, cinnamon stick, star anise, and bay leaves. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  6. Simmer the Pork:
    • Reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Let the pork braise for about 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the pork is tender and the sauce has thickened slightly. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  7. Thicken the Sauce (Optional):
    • If you want a thicker sauce, you can mix 1 tablespoon of cornstarch with a bit of cold water to make a slurry. Stir it into the pot and let it cook for an additional 5 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  8. Season:
    • Taste the braised pork and season with salt and pepper as needed. Remove the cinnamon stick, star anise, and bay leaves.
  9. Serve:
    • Garnish with chopped green onions (optional) and serve the braised pork with steamed rice or noodles.
