Turon (Banana Spring Rolls)

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe saba bananas, sliced into strips
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 10 spring roll wrappers
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Banana:
    • Sprinkle brown sugar on the banana slices.
  2. Wrap the Bananas:
    • Roll the bananas in spring roll wrappers, folding the edges in as you go.
  3. Fry the Rolls:
    • Heat oil in a pan and fry the rolls until golden and crispy.
  4. Drain and Serve:
    • Drain on paper towels and serve warm.

Enjoy this crispy and sweet treat for a delightful snack!
