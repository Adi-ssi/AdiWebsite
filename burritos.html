Bean Burritos

Ingredients

  • 1 can refried beans
  • 4 flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • Salsa

Instructions

  1. Warm the tortillas and refried beans.
  2. Spread beans on each tortilla, top with cheese and salsa.
  3. Roll up the tortillas and serve.
