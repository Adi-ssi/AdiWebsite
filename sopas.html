Sopas (Filipino Creamy Chicken Soup)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb chicken breast, cooked and shredded
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup elbow macaroni or small pasta
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Vegetables:
    • In a pot, sauté onions and carrots in butter until softened.
  2. Prepare the Soup Base:
    • Add chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  3. Cook the Pasta:
    • Stir in macaroni and cook until tender.
  4. Add Chicken and Milk:
    • Add shredded chicken and evaporated milk, then simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Season:
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste, then serve warm.

Enjoy this comforting Filipino soup, perfect for rainy days!
