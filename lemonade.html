Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh lemon juice (about 4 lemons)
  • 5 cups water
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Combine Ingredients:
    • In a pitcher, combine lemon juice, sugar, and water.
  2. Stir:
    • Stir until the sugar is dissolved.
  3. Chill:
    • Add ice cubes and chill in the refrigerator.
  4. Serve:
    • Serve cold with lemon slices for garnish.

Enjoy this refreshing, tangy lemonade!
