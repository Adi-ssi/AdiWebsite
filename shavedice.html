Shaved Ice (with Toppings)

Ingredients

  • Shaved ice (or crushed ice)
  • Sweetened condensed milk or any syrup of choice
  • Fruit toppings (like mango, strawberries, or bananas)
  • Sweetened red beans (optional)

Instructions

  1. Fill a Bowl with Shaved Ice:
    • Place shaved ice (or crushed ice) into a bowl.
  2. Drizzle with Milk or Syrup:
    • Drizzle sweetened condensed milk or syrup over the ice.
  3. Top with Fruits and Beans:
    • Top with your choice of fruit (mango, strawberries, bananas) and optionally, sweetened red beans.
  4. Serve Immediately:
    • Serve immediately for a cooling treat!

Shaved ice with toppings is a refreshing and customizable dessert perfect for hot days!
