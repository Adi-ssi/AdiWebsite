Lumpia (Filipino Spring Rolls)

Ingredients

  • 1 pack lumpia wrappers
  • 1/2 lb ground pork or beef
  • 1 small carrot, grated
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup green beans, finely chopped
  • 1 egg (to seal)
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Cook the Meat:
    • In a pan, cook ground meat until browned.
  2. Sauté the Vegetables:
    • Add onions, carrots, and green beans, then sauté until softened.
  3. Cool the Mixture:
    • Let the mixture cool before wrapping it in lumpia wrappers.
  4. Wrap the Lumpia:
    • Roll up the filling tightly and seal with a beaten egg.
  5. Fry the Lumpia:
    • Fry the lumpia until golden and crispy, then drain on paper towels.
  6. Serve:
    • Serve with sweet and sour sauce.

Enjoy this crispy Filipino favorite as an appetizer or snack!
