Ginger Lemon Tea

Ingredients

  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, peeled and sliced
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tbsp honey (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil the Ginger:
    • In a saucepan, boil water with the ginger slices for 10 minutes.
  2. Add the Lemon:
    • Add the lemon slices and simmer for another 2-3 minutes.
  3. Strain and Sweeten:
    • Strain the tea into a cup and stir in honey if desired.
  4. Serve:
    • Serve warm or chilled.

Enjoy this soothing and refreshing Ginger Lemon Tea!
