Milk Tea

Ingredients

  • 2 black tea bags
  • 2 cups hot water
  • 1/2 cup milk (or non-dairy milk)
  • 2 tbsp sugar or sweetener (adjust to taste)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Brew the Tea:
    • Steep the tea bags in hot water for 5 minutes.
  2. Sweeten the Tea:
    • Remove the tea bags and stir in sugar until dissolved.
  3. Add Milk:
    • Add milk and stir to combine.
  4. Cool or Serve with Ice:
    • Let it cool or serve with ice for a chilled milk tea.

Enjoy this creamy and refreshing milk tea!
