Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter or oil

Instructions

  1. Whisk the eggs with salt and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Heat a pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan and cook, stirring frequently, until scrambled and cooked through.
  4. Serve immediately.
