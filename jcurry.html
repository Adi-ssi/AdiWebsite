Japanese Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) chicken (or beef/pork), cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 3 cups water or chicken broth
  • 1 pack (100g) Japanese curry roux (available in blocks or granules, such as S&B or Golden Curry)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:
    • Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onions and cook until they turn translucent, about 3-4 minutes.
  2. Cook the Meat:
    • Add the chicken (or your choice of meat) to the pot. Cook until the meat is lightly browned on all sides.
  3. Add the Vegetables:
    • Add the carrots and potatoes to the pot, stirring to combine with the meat and onions. Season with a little salt and pepper.
  4. Add Liquids:
    • Pour in the water or chicken broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 20 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Add the Curry Roux:
    • Break the curry roux into pieces and stir it into the pot. Continue to simmer for another 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally until the curry thickens and becomes smooth.
  6. Adjust Seasoning:
    • Taste the curry and adjust seasoning, adding more salt, pepper, or a little sugar if desired for balance.
  7. Serve:
    • Serve the Japanese curry over a bed of steamed rice.
