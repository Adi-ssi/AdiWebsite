Arroz Caldo

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into serving pieces (or 2-3 chicken thighs)
  • 1 1/2 cups rice (preferably glutinous rice or regular rice)
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced into strips
  • 8 cups water or chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2-3 hard-boiled eggs (optional)
  • 1-2 green onions, chopped (for garnish)
  • Fried garlic (for garnish)
  • Calamansi or lemon wedges (for serving)
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Sauté the aromatics: In a large pot, heat the cooking oil over medium heat. Add the garlic, onion, and ginger. Sauté until the onion becomes soft and translucent, and the garlic is fragrant, about 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add the chicken: Add the chicken pieces to the pot. Sauté until the chicken is lightly browned on all sides.
  3. Add the rice and broth: Add the rice to the pot, stirring for a couple of minutes to combine with the chicken and aromatics. Pour in the water or chicken broth and bring it to a boil.
  4. Simmer: Once it starts boiling, reduce the heat and let it simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the rice has softened and the porridge thickens to your desired consistency. Stir occasionally to prevent the rice from sticking to the bottom.
  5. Season: Add the fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning if needed.
  6. Serve: Once the chicken is cooked through and the porridge is creamy and thick, serve it in bowls. Garnish with hard-boiled eggs, green onions, fried garlic, and a wedge of calamansi or lemon on the side.
