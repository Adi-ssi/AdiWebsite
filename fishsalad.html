Tuna Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tuna
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced celery

Instructions

  1. Drain the tuna and place it in a bowl.
  2. Mix with mayonnaise, salt, pepper, and diced celery.
  3. Serve with crackers or on a sandwich.
