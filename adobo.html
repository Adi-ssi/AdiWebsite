Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 tsp peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken:
    • In a pot, combine chicken, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, peppercorns, and sugar. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Brown the Chicken:
    • Heat oil in a pan, then brown the chicken on all sides.
  3. Simmer:
    • Add the marinade to the pan and simmer for 30 minutes or until the chicken is tender and the sauce thickens.
  4. Serve:
    • Serve with steamed rice.

Enjoy this classic Filipino dish, full of savory flavors!
