Iced Coffee

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brewed coffee (chilled)
  • 1/2 cup milk or cream
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Brew the Coffee:
    • Brew your coffee and allow it to cool to room temperature, then refrigerate it until chilled.
  2. Mix the Ingredients:
    • In a glass, combine the chilled coffee, milk, and sugar.
  3. Add Ice:
    • Add ice cubes and stir well.
  4. Serve and Enjoy:
    • Serve and enjoy!

Perfect for a cool and energizing treat!
