Fresh Mango with Sticky Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup sticky rice (glutinous rice)
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Cook the Sticky Rice:
    • Cook sticky rice according to instructions and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Coconut Milk Mixture:
    • In a saucepan, heat coconut milk, sugar, and salt. Stir until the sugar dissolves.
  3. Combine the Rice and Coconut Milk:
    • Pour the coconut milk mixture over the sticky rice and mix well.
  4. Serve:
    • Serve the sticky rice with fresh mango slices on the side.
  5. Enjoy this delicious and creamy Thai-inspired dessert!

This dessert is perfect for a light and refreshing treat, combining the sweetness of mango and the richness of coconut sticky rice!
