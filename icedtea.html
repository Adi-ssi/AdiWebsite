Classic Iced Tea

Ingredients

  • 4 black tea bags
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/4 cup sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon slices (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil the Water:
    • Boil 4 cups of water, then pour over the tea bags in a heatproof container.
  2. Steep the Tea:
    • Let the tea steep for 5-10 minutes, then remove the tea bags.
  3. Sweeten (Optional):
    • If desired, stir in sugar until dissolved.
  4. Chill:
    • Refrigerate the tea to chill.
  5. Serve:
    • Serve over ice with lemon slices, if preferred.

Enjoy a refreshing glass of iced tea perfect for hot days!
