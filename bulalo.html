Bulalo

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef shank (with bone marrow)
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 8 cups water
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and cut into quarters
  • 2 corn cobs, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 large cabbage, quartered
  • 1 bunch bok choy or pechay
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1/2 tsp of whole peppercorns
  • Optional: 2 tbsp soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Prepare the beef shank: Wash the beef shank well. In a large pot, add the beef shank and cover with water. Bring to a boil over medium heat and then reduce to a simmer. Skim off any scum or impurities that float to the surface. Continue to simmer for 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the meat becomes tender and the bone marrow softens.
  2. Add aromatics and flavoring: Add the onions, garlic, fish sauce, and soy sauce (optional) to the pot. Simmer for another 30 minutes.
  3. Add the vegetables: Add the potatoes and corn to the pot. Continue simmering for another 20 minutes until the potatoes are tender.
  4. Season: Taste the broth and season with salt and pepper. Add whole peppercorns if you like a bit of extra spice.
  5. Finish the dish: Add the cabbage and bok choy (pechay) in the last 10 minutes of cooking. Simmer until the cabbage is tender but not mushy.
  6. Serve: Ladle the soup and meat into bowls, ensuring each serving gets some of the marrow from the beef bones. Serve hot with steamed rice on the side.
