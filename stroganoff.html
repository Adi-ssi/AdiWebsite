Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients

  • 500g (1 lb) beef (sirloin, tenderloin, or stew beef), sliced thinly into strips
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard (optional)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (optional, for extra richness)
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon paprika (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Egg noodles or rice (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Beef:
    • Slice the beef into thin strips, cutting against the grain. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Sear the Beef:
    • In a large skillet or pan, heat the olive oil and 1 tablespoon of butter over medium-high heat. Add the beef in batches, making sure not to overcrowd the pan. Sear the beef for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until browned. Remove the beef from the pan and set it aside.
  3. Cook the Onion and Garlic:
    • In the same pan, add the remaining tablespoon of butter. Add the chopped onion and cook over medium heat for 3-4 minutes, until softened. Add the garlic and cook for an additional 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Make the Sauce:
    • Sprinkle the flour over the onions and garlic, stirring to coat. Cook for 1-2 minutes to form a roux (this thickens the sauce). Gradually pour in the beef broth while stirring to prevent lumps from forming. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 3-5 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken.
  5. Add Sour Cream and Mustard:
    • Stir in the sour cream and Dijon mustard (if using). Let the sauce simmer for another 3-4 minutes, then return the beef to the pan. Stir to combine and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, just until the beef is heated through.
  6. Adjust Consistency:
    • If the sauce is too thick, add a little heavy cream or more broth to reach your desired consistency. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika to taste.
  7. Serve:
    • Serve the beef stroganoff over a bed of egg noodles, rice, or mashed potatoes. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.
