Frozen Yogurt or Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries (or mango)
  • 1/2 cup milk or juice
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional)

Instructions

  1. Blend Ingredients:
    • In a blender, combine the Greek yogurt, frozen fruit, and milk (or juice). Blend until smooth.
  2. Sweeten (Optional):
    • If desired, sweeten the mixture with honey and blend again until well combined.
  3. Serve Immediately:
    • Pour the smoothie or yogurt into bowls or glasses and serve right away as a refreshing treat!

Enjoy this chilled and healthy treat perfect for warm weather!
