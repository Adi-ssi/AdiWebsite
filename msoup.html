Mushroom Soup

Ingredients

  • 500g (about 1 lb) fresh mushrooms (button mushrooms, cremini, or a mix)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups vegetable broth (or chicken broth)
  • 1 cup heavy cream or milk (for a lighter version)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh thyme or parsley (optional, for garnish)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (optional, for sautéing)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Mushrooms:
    • Clean the mushrooms with a damp cloth or paper towel to remove any dirt. Slice them thinly.
  2. Sauté the Aromatics:
    • In a large pot, heat the butter (and olive oil, if using) over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Cook the Mushrooms:
    • Add the sliced mushrooms to the pot and cook, stirring occasionally, until the mushrooms release their moisture and reduce in size, about 8-10 minutes. The mushrooms should be well-browned.
  4. Make the Roux:
    • Sprinkle the flour over the mushrooms and stir to coat. Cook for 1-2 minutes to form a roux and remove the raw taste of the flour.
  5. Add Broth and Simmer:
    • Gradually pour in the vegetable or chicken broth while stirring to combine. Bring the soup to a simmer and cook for about 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Add Cream:
    • Stir in the heavy cream or milk and let it simmer for another 5 minutes. If you prefer a thicker soup, you can use an immersion blender to blend part or all of the soup to your desired consistency. Alternatively, you can leave it chunky for more texture.
  7. Season:
    • Taste the soup and season with salt and pepper as needed.
  8. Serve:
    • Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh thyme or parsley if desired. Serve with crusty bread or croutons on the side.
