Cheesy Egg Muffins

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped spinach

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Whisk the eggs in a bowl and add cheese, spinach, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the mixture into muffin tins.
  4. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the muffins are set and golden.
