Mango Graham Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and sliced
  • 1 pack (200g) graham crackers
  • 1 can (300ml) condensed milk
  • 1 can (300ml) all-purpose cream
  • 1/2 cup fresh milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar (optional, adjust based on mango sweetness)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Cream Mixture:
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the condensed milk, all-purpose cream, and fresh milk. Stir well to combine until smooth. If you prefer it sweeter, add the sugar and mix until dissolved.
  2. Layer the Graham Crackers:
    • In a rectangular baking dish (or a dish of your choice), arrange a layer of graham crackers at the bottom. You can break the crackers as needed to fit the size of your dish.
  3. Add the Cream Layer:
    • Pour about one-third of the cream mixture over the layer of graham crackers, spreading it evenly with a spatula.
  4. Add Mangoes:
    • Place a layer of fresh mango slices over the cream mixture. Arrange them in a single layer to ensure the mangoes are evenly distributed.
  5. Repeat the Layers:
    • Repeat the layers: another layer of graham crackers, followed by another layer of cream mixture, and another layer of mango slices. Continue layering until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of the cream mixture on top.
  6. Chill:
    • Refrigerate the cake for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld and the cake to set.
  7. Serve:
    • Before serving, garnish with additional mango slices on top for a fresh touch.
  8. Slice and serve chilled!

Enjoy this refreshing, tropical dessert perfect for any occasion!
