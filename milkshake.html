Strawberry Milkshake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh or frozen strawberries
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Blend the Ingredients:
    • Blend strawberries, ice cream, milk, and sugar in a blender until smooth.
  2. Serve:
    • Pour into glasses and serve with whipped cream or a cherry on top, if desired.

Enjoy this creamy and refreshing strawberry milkshake!
