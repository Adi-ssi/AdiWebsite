Kalamansi Juice (Filipino Lemonade)

Ingredients

  • 10-12 kalamansi (Filipino lime) fruits
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Squeeze the Kalamansi:
    • Cut the kalamansi fruits in half and squeeze out the juice into a pitcher.
  2. Add Water and Sugar:
    • Add water and sugar, then stir until the sugar dissolves.
  3. Chill and Serve:
    • Chill in the refrigerator or serve over ice cubes for a refreshing drink.

Enjoy the tangy, citrusy goodness of this Filipino classic!
