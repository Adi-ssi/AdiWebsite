Veggie Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 cup broth
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/2 cup pasta (optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine diced tomatoes, broth, and vegetables in a pot.
  2. Simmer for 20 minutes, then add pasta if desired and cook until tender.
