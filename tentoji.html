Tentoji Don

Ingredients

  • 1 boneless pork loin chop (for tonkatsu)
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • 1 cup cooked sushi rice or short-grain rice (for the base)
  • 4-5 shrimp, peeled and deveined (for tempura)
  • 1 small sweet potato, sliced thinly (for tempura)
  • 1/2 zucchini, sliced (for tempura)

Tempura Batter

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1 egg

For the Sauces and Garnishes

  • Soy sauce (for tonkatsu sauce or tempura dipping)
  • Tonkatsu sauce (for tonkatsu)
  • Lemon wedges (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Tonkatsu:
    • Season the pork chop with salt and pepper.
    • Dredge the pork chop in flour, dip it in beaten egg, then coat it with panko breadcrumbs, pressing gently to adhere.
    • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Fry the breaded pork chop for about 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through. Drain on a paper towel.
  2. Prepare the Tempura:
    • For the tempura batter, mix the flour, cornstarch, cold water, and egg in a bowl until just combined. It should be lumpy, not smooth.
    • Heat oil in a separate pan over medium heat.
    • Dip the shrimp, sweet potato, and zucchini slices into the tempura batter, allowing excess batter to drip off.
    • Fry the battered vegetables and shrimp in the hot oil until golden and crispy (about 2-3 minutes). Drain on paper towels.
  3. Assemble the Tentoji Don:
    • Place a serving of cooked rice in a bowl.
    • Slice the tonkatsu pork into strips and place it on top of the rice.
    • Arrange the tempura shrimp and vegetables on top of the rice and tonkatsu.
    • Drizzle tonkatsu sauce over the tonkatsu and soy sauce or tempura dipping sauce over the tempura, as desired.
  4. Serve:
    • Garnish with lemon wedges if you like and enjoy your hearty Tentoji Don!
