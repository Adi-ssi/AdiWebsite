Chilled Coconut Water

Ingredients

  • Fresh coconut water (or store-bought)

Instructions

  1. Pour the Coconut Water:
    • Pour the chilled coconut water into a glass.
  2. Optional Ice:
    • Optionally, add a few ice cubes for extra coolness.
  3. Serve:
    • Serve and enjoy the refreshing hydration.

Chilled coconut water is a naturally refreshing and hydrating drink, perfect for cooling off on a hot day.
