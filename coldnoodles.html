Cold Noodles (Pancit or Soba)

Ingredients

  • 200g soba noodles or rice noodles
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Cook the Noodles:
    • Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. Toss with Soy Sauce and Sesame Oil:
    • Toss the noodles with soy sauce and sesame oil until well coated.
  3. Add Vegetables:
    • Add cucumber and avocado slices. Mix gently.
  4. Serve:
    • Serve cold and enjoy this refreshing noodle dish!

This dish is perfect for a light, cool meal on hot days, combining fresh veggies with the savory taste of soy sauce and sesame oil.
